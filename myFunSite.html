Fun Facts About UW

The Most Beautiful College Campus

  • Fact1

    The Wave

    The idea of the fan cheer known as the Wave was conceived at UW in 1981

  • Fact2

    Inventions

    Bubble gum, vinyl, synthetic rubber, and the color TV tube were all invented at UW.

  • Fact3

    Loudest college football game

    UW holds the record for the loudest college football game which was set at 133.6 decibels in 1992 against Nebraska.

  • Fact4

    Mount Ranier

    The University of Washington owns all the airspace from its campus to Mount Ranier to ensure that no buildings will disrupt the view from Red Square to the mountain.
